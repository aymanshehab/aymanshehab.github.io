From Questions to Insight:
My Journey into Data

I’ve always been drawn to systems, how things work, how they connect, and how small signals can lead to big outcomes. Over time, that curiosity found its place in the world of data.
For me, data analysis is more than just numbers and charts. it’s a way to explore problems, uncover meaning, and support better decisions. It allows me to combine logic with creativity, structure with flexibility, and continuous learning with real-world impact.

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Ayman Ali Shehab — a data analyst with a passion for turning raw data into meaningful insights that drive smart decisions.
I hold a strong foundation in SQL, Python, Power BI, Excel, and Tableau, and I’m certified through the Google Data Analytics Certificate and the KPMG Virtual Internship. With years of experience in administration and design, I bring a unique blend of business sense, creativity, and technical skill to every project I work on.
Currently based in Egypt, I work with real-world datasets to solve business problems, automate workflows, and build dashboards that speak clearly. I’m always learning, always building — whether it's a dashboard, a data pipeline, or a story told through numbers.
When I’m not analyzing data, I’m either developing side projects, sharing tutorials on my YouTube channels Data by Shehab and CodeON, or diving into tech, philosophy, and language learning.
